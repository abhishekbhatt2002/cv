Abhishek Bhatt is an independent singer-songwriter, composer, and music producer currently based in Lucknow, India.
He creates music mostly in the pop genre and is well known for the blend of his voice and profound melodies, originating from his own perspective, which offered a sense of relatability to the listeners.

He has a very soothing voice with a strong knowledge of production which make him totally different from the rest of the artists around the world!
